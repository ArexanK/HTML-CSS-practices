Deep dish Brownies

Prep time

60 mins

Servings

9

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup buttter, melted
  • 1 1/2 cups white sugar
  • 1 1/2 cups white sugar
  • 1 1/12 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 3 eggs
  • 3/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup unsweetend cocoa powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder1/2 teaspoon salt

Directions

  1. Preheat over 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) and Grease an 8 inch square pan.
  2. In a large bowl, blend melted butterm sugar and vanilla Beat in eggs one at a time
  3. Combine the flour, coco, Bking powder and salt Graduallt blend into the egg mixture.
  4. Spread the batter into the prepared pan.
  5. Bake in preheated oven for 40 to 45 minutes, or untill brownies begin to pull away from the sides of the pan.
  6. Let brownies cool, then cut into squares. Enjoy!

Tip

Aluminum foil can be used to keep food moist, cook it evenly, and make clean-up easier.
